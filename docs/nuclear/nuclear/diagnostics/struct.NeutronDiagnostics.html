pub fn estimate_k(&mut self) -> Option<(f64, Vec<i64>)>

Estimates the multiplication coefficient k based on data collected in the neutron scheduler during a simulation run. At the end of the simulation, this vector is passed from the scheduler to diagnostics via track_simulation_halt.
